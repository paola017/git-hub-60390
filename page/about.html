About Us

More Information about our park

Welcome to our RV Resort located in southern Tennessee. White Sands RV Resort is brand new with 11 sites that are full hookups that boast all pull-through spaces up to 85 ft long. Set within a beautiful, serene, rural setting in southeastern Tennessee close to numerous attractions (We are just a hop skip and a jump from Pigeon Forge). Set on 65 acres so you can explore and appreciate the natural views, flora and fauna of the area.

The history of Greeneville goes back to 10,0000 BC where natives used the land to hunt and gather until the first Euro-American settlers arrived slightly before 1772. North of the city was also the famous Great Indian War Path. You can also visit the Andrew Johnson Historical Site of the 17th President of the United States, Andrew Johnson, who began his political career in Greeneville.

We welcome your fur babies and they can enjoy our fully enclosed, 3 acre dog park. We also offer a spacious clubhouse that has high speed internet, 2 ADA bathrooms, washers and dryers. We strive to be a quiet venue and this is our focus. Even though the park impresses relaxation; there are numerous nearby attractions, entertainment and everything you would desire to enjoy yourselves during your stay.
